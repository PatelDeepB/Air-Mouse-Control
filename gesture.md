# AirMouse++ Gesture Mappings

This document explains the default gestures configured for AirMouse++ and how to perform them.

## Core Gestures

1. **Move Cursor (Point Gesture)**
   - **How to perform:** Point your **index finger** up, keeping your other fingers closed (your thumb can be open or closed). 
   - **What it does:** Moves the laptop's mouse cursor.
   - **Tip:** To stop the mouse from moving (for example, if you want to click), you can transition to a Pinch.

2. **Left Click (Pinch Gesture)**
   - **How to perform:** Bring the tips of your **thumb and index finger** together so they touch. Keep your other fingers closed.
   - **What it does:** Triggers a standard Left Mouse Click.

3. **Play / Pause Media (Fist Gesture)**
   - **How to perform:** Close all your fingers to make a **fist**.
   - **What it does:** Plays or pauses your current music/video playing in the background (like Spotify or YouTube).

4. **Next Track (Peace Gesture)**
   - **How to perform:** Hold up your **index and middle finger** in a "Peace" / "V" sign. Keep the ring and pinky fingers closed.
   - **What it does:** Skips to the next track on your media player.

## Changing Mappings
If you want to swap what these gestures do, just open `config.yaml` in the root folder and change the mapping! For example, you can map `peace` to `middle_click` or `mute`.
